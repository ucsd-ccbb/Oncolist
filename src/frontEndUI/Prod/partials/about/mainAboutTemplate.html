Oncolist

A gene-centric search
engine for cancer

Oncolist is a search engine which ranks gene networks, conditions, publication
authors, and drugs in cancer datasets derived from The Cancer Genome Atlas (TCGA)
and the Gene Expression Omnibus (GEO)

Harvesting

We gather data from
TCGA, GEO, ClinVar,
DrugBank, COSMIC and
PubMed

Processing

Our engineers develop
algorithms which are applied
through cloud computing.
Data is indexed and
stored in thecloud.

Scoring

A scoring algorithm is
used to filter and
rank your results.
